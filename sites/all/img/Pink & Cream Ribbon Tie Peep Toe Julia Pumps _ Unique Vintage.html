Details

Julia is a vision! These picturesque mary jane pumps boast a pink and cream color blocked design dotted with perforation detail, a 3'' stacked heel, grosgrain ribbon tie, cushion insole and grosgrain ribbon tie for a secure fit. Crafted from man-made materials, Julia is a retro dream waiting to come true!
Please note all shoes have a 4 day handling time before they will ship from our warehouse and there is a 15% restocking fee if you choose to return.

FEATURES:
Imported
3" Heel
Synthetic Materials
Grosgrain Ribbon Tie
No Tread/Little Traction
Runs true to size according to Chelsea Crew brand sizing.
Chelsea Crew brand shoe size 36 corresponds to US size 5; 37 to 6; 38 to 7; 39 to 8; 40 to 9; 41 to 10.
